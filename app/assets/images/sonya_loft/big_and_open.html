Features

Description

Summit Lofts is a boutique collection of nine new construction Lofts in the heart of Columbia Waterfront in Carrol Gardens.

This absolutely stunning, 1475 sq. ft. LOFT features a very large open loft living room area with a wall of South-facing windows. The open kitchen is equipped with quartz countertops, Edison bulb pendulum lamps, brand new stainless steel appliances including dishwasher, and a stove top pot filler for cooking convenience. The unit has two bedrooms (one QUEEN one KING) with abundant sunlight plus another room that can be as a third bedroom or home office. Two designer bathrooms (one with bathtub, one with shower) with marble finishes and vintage tiling complement the "old meets new" aesthetic. Washer/dryer is in the unit, plus extensive storage with large closets. Central heating and air, a central vacuum for easy cleaning, cameras with video intercom and HEATED FLOORS for year-round comfort add to the impressive list of amenities in the building. Apartment 1 also has a large private backyard! Unbeatable views, unparalleled architecture- the owners have poured heart and soul into this construction and the resulting work is a design and construction masterpiece!
Nestled between Carroll Gardens and Red Hook, the Columbia Waterfront District offers the perfect balance in Brooklyn living. Community gardens and tree lined streets give a small town feel just four subway stops from Manhattan, while James Beard award winning restaurants and acoustic music venues enhance the area's nightlife. The newly redesigned Brooklyn Bridge Park is only a few blocks away. It is a quiet neighborhood with stoop sales in the Spring and street fairs in the Summer, a serene refuge in the heart of New York.
